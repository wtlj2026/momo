Blog - Life Updates

April 4th, 2026

Hi, I'm Mochi. I Have Acquired Humans.

Today was a big day.

My new family claimed they "weren't getting a pet." Very funny. A few cute photos later, the whole committee approved me immediately. Excellent decision.

Within three hours, I had a new home, food, supplies, and a name: Mochi. Soft, sweet, and slightly chewy-sounding. I approve.

When I arrived, I bravely inspected my new kingdom from the safest possible location: under the bed.

The humans tried everything to see me—soft voices, snacks, dramatic floor-crawling. Impressive effort. Not effective, but impressive.

For now, I remain mysterious, fluffy, and unavailable.

Tomorrow, maybe I'll come out.

Or maybe I'll continue my important work as Bed Basement Manager.

Purrs,
Mochi 🐾
